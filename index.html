Welcome to Rindali Maldives Maaenboodhoo, an exclusive guest house on the island of Maaenboodhoo in Dhaalu Atoll, the central atoll of the Maldives. With 6 beautifully designed rooms, including five doubles and a spacious family room, Rindali can accommodate up to 14 guests. As the only hotel on the island, Rindali offers an intimate and unique experience, allowing you to immerse yourself in the tranquility of Maaenboodhoo. Located 178.8 km away from Male, the capital city, our bed and breakfast service ensures a comfortable and delightful stay. Enjoy the luxury of a private bikini beach reserved exclusively for Rindali guests, providing a serene spot to bask in the sun and relish the beauty of the Maldivian turquoise waters.
